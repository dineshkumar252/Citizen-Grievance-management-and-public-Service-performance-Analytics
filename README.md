 Citizen Grievance Management & Public Service Performance Analytics System
 Project Overview

The Citizen Grievance Management & Public Service Performance Analytics System is a Business Analytics project designed to improve the monitoring and management of citizen complaints received by government departments. The project leverages Microsoft Excel for data preparation and Power BI for interactive dashboard development to provide meaningful insights into complaint resolution, departmental performance, officer productivity, citizen satisfaction, SLA compliance, and cost analysis.

Government organizations often receive a large number of complaints related to public services such as water supply, roads, sanitation, electricity, waste management, and other civic issues. Due to manual processes and fragmented reporting systems, decision-makers face challenges in monitoring complaint status, tracking departmental performance, identifying delayed resolutions, and optimizing operational costs.

This project addresses these challenges by creating a centralized analytics dashboard that enables government officials and stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify bottlenecks, and make data-driven decisions to improve public service delivery.

 Project Objectives
Develop an interactive dashboard for monitoring citizen complaints.
Analyze complaint trends across departments and districts.
Measure complaint resolution performance using SLA metrics.
Evaluate department and officer performance.
Analyze citizen satisfaction through ratings and reopened complaints.
Compare estimated and actual complaint resolution costs.
Support data-driven decision-making for government authorities.
Business Problem

Government departments face several operational challenges in grievance management:

Lack of centralized complaint monitoring.
Delayed complaint resolution.
High SLA (Service Level Agreement) breaches.
Difficulty in tracking officer performance.
Low visibility into district-wise complaint trends.
Poor citizen satisfaction due to delayed services.
Increasing operational costs without performance analysis.
Proposed Solution

This project provides an interactive Power BI solution that enables decision-makers to:

Monitor complaints in real time.
Track complaint resolution status.
Identify departments with high complaint volumes.
Evaluate officer productivity and workload.
Monitor SLA compliance and delayed complaints.
Analyze citizen satisfaction and reopened cases.
Compare estimated and actual operational costs.
Generate actionable insights for improving public service delivery.
